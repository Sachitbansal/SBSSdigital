Robotic Process Automation

Robotic Process Automation (RPA Technology) is one of the most cutting-edge Digital Transformation technological tools which can be used by organizations to cost-cut expense, save time, and maximize resources via the automation of repetitive, high-volume tasks like maintaining and processing physical and digital data and more.

  • A report from the most-known smart device brand Apple shows that iPhones captured an estimated 15% to 20% market penetration rate in 2020 and the outstanding strategy helped Apple to sell 77.3 million iPhones, which was a straight 20 percent hike on their previous years’ sales figure.
  • As per a new study by Grand View Research, the global robotic process automation (RPA) market size is expected to reach USD 3.11 billion by 2025. The global market is estimated to expand at a CAGR of 31.1% during the forecast period. Hence, it’s easy to comprehend the popularity of RPA.
  • RPA involves the use of software with artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning to manage high-volume, repetitive, time-consuming tasks that need humans to execute. Put simply; it’s a software robot!
  • You can describe these bots as virtual workers that run round the clock with minimal downtime based on a defined schedule or triggers. Here at SBSSdigital, we provide you with efficient RPA that helps you in managing, filtering, and maintaining physical and digital data effortlessly.
  • Be it extracting and filtering new data to create productive lead sheets for your business or maintaining track records - our extraordinary RPA gets you covered with everything that’s related to data management or extraction. By saving a considerable amount of time, they help you boost productivity and generate greater predictability and higher process quality.
